I arrived home inside of an UGG sheepskin hat

I was abandoned on a seat on the IRT #1 train inside of a black backpack. The train was halted and the conductor informed of a suspicious package. They cautiously lifted open the backpack and found me amid a slew of restaurant kitchen towels which had the fresh imprints of all of my siblings. I was then rescued by a passenger who snuck me into her office inside an UGG sheepskin hat and eventually...home with her.
